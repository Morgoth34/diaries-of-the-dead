About Diaries of the Dead

Diaries of the Dead is a blog that consists of short stories (with original illustrations) written from the perspective of the lost souls of the dead. In these stories they tell the reader all about the life they once led and why their spirits have not yet crossed over to the afterlife. These stories give the reader an intimate look into the world of the dead.
